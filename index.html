Ajira GIS

Vision

Ajira GIS (AGIS) is a decentralized machine learning system that provides human language interfaces in African languages, and rewards people for training the artificial intelligence to translate these languages. AGIS enables locally targeted services such as personal assistant, financial management, virtual customer support, and other personalized services. AGIS uses a blockchain platform to add human involvement in building the artificial intelligence.


Anyone can participate in the platform and be paid for contributing to the AGIS. Workers can perform digital tasks (e.g. translations, AI training), or provide data to the AGIS in exchange for Ajira Points - the medium of exchange of value for providing or consuming services in the Ajira ecosystem. Businesses will pay to access services such as language translation APIs, and speech-to-text conversion for their applications.

Earn

We connect crowdworkers to digital tasks. You can help train our machine translation system to enable us to bring modern technological advances such as voice-activated services in your local languages.

Digital Tasks

You can earn Ajira Points by translating or verifying other translations in your native language. You can also create text in your language and submit it for verification. All you need is a computer or mobile device with internet access.

How it works:

  1. Sign up to AGIS

  2. Fill out your language preference

  3. Request for a task

  4. Translate the given text or submit language text

  5. Get paid after your work is verified
