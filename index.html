Ajira GIS

A machine learning system for language interfaces driven by blockchain technology

Vision

Ajira GIS (AGIS) is a decentralized machine learning system that provides human language interfaces in African languages, and rewards people for training the artificial intelligence to learn these languages. AGIS enables locally targeted services such as personal assistant, financial management, virtual customer support, and other personalized services. AGIS uses a blockchain platform to add human involvement in building the artificial intelligence.


Anyone can participate in the platform and be paid for contributing to the AGIS. Workers can perform digital tasks (e.g. translations, AI training), or provide data to the AGIS in exchange for Ajira Points - the medium of exchange of value for providing or consuming services in the Ajira ecosystem. Businesses will pay to access services such as language translation APIs, and speech-to-text conversion for their applications.

Earn

We connect crowdworkers to digital tasks. You can help train our machine translation system to enable us to bring modern technological advances such as voice-activated services in your local languages.


Digital Tasks

You can earn Ajira Points by translating or verifying other translations in your native language. You can also create text in your language and submit it for verification. All you need is a computer or mobile device with internet access.

How it works:

  1. Sign up to AGIS

  2. Fill out your language preference

  3. Request for a task

  4. Translate the given text or submit language text

  5. Get paid after your work is verified

Mining

Become an AGIS processing or storage provider (miner) to generate income. In order to teach the AI all these languages and to gradually improve its models, it will need a lot of data, compute power and connectivity. The Machine Learning neural network operation is decentralized and distributed over nodes through blockchain technology. By decentralizing the AI network, participants can provide computing and storage nodes to the AGIS network, and be paid for their AI resource contribution. Miners can install the AGIS software and join the network to mine Ajira Points, which will be rewarded for compute processing and storage capacity. The more compute and storage resources are offered to the shared AGIS ecosystem, the faster the AI training, and the more Ajira Points are rewarded.

Developers

AGIS is an open-source product that aims to innovate more effectively and solve shared problems. We invite developers to participate in the Ajira community and earn Ajira Points for their development contribution to the project. Once your code is reviewed and accepted into the official development branch, you will receive a bounty in Ajira Points. The AGIS source is hosted on Github

Business

Businesses that need to translate content can integrate their applications easily with simple programmatic interfaces for low-cost localization solutions. AGIS will provide fast and dynamic translation services through translation APIs that support African languages.

Businesses will benefit from accessing our machine translation services at a competitive cost. AGIS makes the Machine Learning neural network operation decentralized and distributed over nodes through blockchain technology. This reduces the computing costs compared to traditional centralized AI computing servers, hence passing on the savings to businesses.

For more information, please contact us.

Ajira Points

Ajira Points are the medium of exchange for consuming or providing services in the AGIS ecosystem. Workers earn Ajira Points when they perform translations and contribute language data to the artificial intelligence. Miners earn Ajira Points by contributing AI compute and storage resources. Businesses will pay for translation services using Ajira Points.

Ajira Points can be exchanged for fiat currency, cryptocurrencies, and loyalty points. Ajira Points can be held in a cryptocurrency wallet that allows owners to store tokens.
